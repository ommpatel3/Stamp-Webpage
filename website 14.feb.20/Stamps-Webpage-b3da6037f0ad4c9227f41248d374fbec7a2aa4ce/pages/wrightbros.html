Stamps  Of  The  World

Showcasing a wide range of stamps from all corners of the world and periods of modern history

Country: United States of America

Issued: 1978

Denomination: 31 US cents

This stamp features an image of Orville and Wilbur Wright, the brothers whose first flight in December 1903 began the era of heavier-than-air aviation. This aircraft, called the Wright Flyer was flown on December 17, 1903 near Kitty Hawk, North Carolina and flew for about 12 seconds, 36 meters. As the brothers couldn't intially decide who would fly the plane, they flipped a coin and Wilbur ended up winning. The stamp's print is lithograohed onto the paper, which gives it the unique graphic style.
