Country: Union of Soviet Socialist Republics

Issued: 1970

Denomination: 6 Soviet kopeks

This Soviet stamp features an image of Vladimir Lenin, along with writing which reads, "1970 world youth meeting dedicated to the birthday of V.I.Lenin". Vladimir Ilyich Ulyanov, better known as Lenin (1870-1924) was a Russian revolutionary, politician, and political theorist. He served as head of government of Soviet Russia from 1917 to 1924 and of the Soviet Union from 1922 to 1924. Lenin is most known for leading the October Revolution, through which the Provisoinal Russian Government was overthrown, and the Bolsheviks, led by Lenin gained power, establishing the Soviet Union.
