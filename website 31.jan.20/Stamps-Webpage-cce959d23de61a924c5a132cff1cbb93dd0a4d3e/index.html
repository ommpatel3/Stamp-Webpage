Stamps  Of  The  World

Showcasing a wide range of stamps from all corners of the world and periods of modern history

Postage has existed since the times of Ancient Egypt. However, as times changed and distances got larger, a way to pay for postage was needed.

In 1840, The Stamp was first invented. It took the form of the British Penny Black

Within almost 200 years, stamps have taken many different forms and have become a medium to document events, commemmorate people and express what makes acountry unique.
