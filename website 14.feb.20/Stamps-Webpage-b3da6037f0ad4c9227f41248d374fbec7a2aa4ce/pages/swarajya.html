Stamps  Of  The  World

Showcasing a wide range of stamps from all corners of the world and periods of modern history

Country: Republic of India

Issued: 1988

Denomination: 60 Indian paise

Two connecting Stamps from India issued to commemorate 40 years of independence. They feature the independence movement leader Bal Gangadhar Tilak (1856-1920) and his famous saying, “swarajya (self rule) is my birthright and I shall have it”. Also included is this same quote written in his native Marathi. Bal Gangadhar Tilak was the first leader of the Indian independence movement and before Gandhi, he was the most widely known political leader. Infact, during his career, he was referred to by the British as “the father of the Indian unrest”. As a result of his oratory skills, he is often referred to as “lokmanya”, roughly meaning “one who is followed by the people”.
