Country: United Arab Republic

Issued: 1964

Denomination: 35 Egyptian millieme

This stamp features an image of Nefertari, the queen of king Ramses the Great of Egypt. The cartouche in the hieroglyphics beside her contains her name. The United Arab Republic was formed in 1958, initially a political union which existed between Syria and Egypt, which is quite unusual considering both countries are not connected by land or sea. This republic was led by the famous Gamal Abdul Nasser who is known through Egypt and the Arab world for overthrowing the monarchy in 1952, modernizing Egypt, and being a large advocate of pan-Arabism. The union lasted until 1961, when the Syrian government fell after a coup d’état, leaving behind a rump state. Egypt continued to officially use the name “UAR” for itself until 1971
