Postage

Has existed since the times of Ancient Egypt. However, as times changed and distances got larger, a way to pay for postage was needed.

In 1840

The Stamp

was first invented. It took the form of the British Penny Black stamp

Ever since then, stamps have taken many forms and have become a medium to document events, commemmorate people and express what makes a country unique.
