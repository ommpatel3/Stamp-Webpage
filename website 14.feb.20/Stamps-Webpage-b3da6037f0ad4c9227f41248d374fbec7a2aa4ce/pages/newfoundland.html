Showcasing a wide range of stamps from all corners of the world and periods of modern history

Country: Dominion of Newfoundland

Issued: 1923

Denomination: 3 cents

Now a province of Canada, Newfoundland remained an independant country from 1907 until the year 1949. This particular stamp features the statue ”the Fighting Newfoundlander” , as a tribute to the Newfoundlanders who fought in the Great War. The Fighting Newfoundlander is a statue of Cpl. Thomas A. Pittman, a native of Little Bay East in Fortune Bay, N.L, who saw action in the First World War in the battle of the Somme and Cambrai.
